During University's Period

I graduated from Soochow University and majored in Big Data Management, where I focused on data analysis and programming, and I actively participated in various competitions like the Hua Nan Financial Holdings competition, NTU Hackathon, Asia Hackathon, and Chunghwa Post Big Data competition. These experiences made me realize my strong interest in front-end engineering and user experience, motivating me to delve into graphic design, web design, and user experience, both inside and outside of school.

First Full-time Job

After graduation, I joined Hua Nan Bank as an information security officer, and this position taught me valuable interpersonal skills, responsibility, and knowledge related to information security. During my tenure at Hua Nan Bank, I continued to learn about user experience and project management, and I aspired to further specialize and work in these domains in the future.

Stage of Exploring

After my resignation, I joined the National Chiao Tung University lab, engaging in research related to human-computer interaction, and this allowed me to deepen my understanding of the field and strengthen my research skills. During this period, I volunteered for UXTW courses, assisting in course preparation. I have coordinated and contributed to five courses so far, enhancing my ability to plan courses and communicate with other volunteers and board members.

Intern

From mid-2020 to the present, I've interned at BenQ Design Center, Garmin, and PDIS (Digital Minister's Office), focusing on user experience design and research. These experiences have honed my skills in user research, market analysis, cross-team collaboration, and communication, as well as product interface design.

During Master's Period

In 2021, I enrolled in the Department of Industrial Design at National Cheng Kung University to continue my studies in human factors. My experiences at NCKU have enriched my research capabilities, my understanding of market product analysis, cross-disciplinary collaboration, specialized knowledge in the medical field, and effective communication and expression skills.
