Details

My internship at BenQ involved two main projects: Content Management System and Device Management System, both of which are primarily B2B products. Throughout the internship, I was guided by two mentors and collaborated with other designers, researchers, PMs, engineers, as well as international sales and vendors.
For the Device Management System project, we started by brainstorming solutions for the issues raised by the PM. We delved into the suggestions provided by clients to the PM, gaining insights into the context and problems users encountered while using this backend management system. Due to time constraints, we couldn't conduct in-depth research and analysis, so we quickly conducted desktop research, competitive analysis, and completed system design. In the end, I documented the design for engineers to develop, and received positive feedback from the PM and clients.
Most of my time during the internship was dedicated to research, analysis, design, and experimentation for the CMS system. As our team mainly followed an agile development approach, each stage of progress was quite fast, allowing for swift feedback from supervisors, clients, and PMs. This approach also fostered close communication and interaction with colleagues from other units throughout the internship.
In the beginning of this project, I conducted usability testing to understand the issues and conditions of the existing product. Through extensive competitive research and analysis, I identified the entry points for our product. This led to the initial product design. Subsequently, I interviewed domestic and international clients to gather feedback on the issues with the existing product and the initial product concept. We translated this feedback into design and conducted in-house product experiments after completing the design. Based on the experimental results, we refined the design to create a comprehensive design proposal, which was used by visual designers and engineers for further development.

Simultaneously, we redesigned the existing product webpages, optimized webpage SEO, and conducted product marketing analysis.
