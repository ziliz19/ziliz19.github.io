Details

This website was created as part of my Introduction to Computer Science course, and I came across the concept of eco-eating in online articles. Therefore, during the initial brainstorming phase of the course project, I chose this concept as the core of the website. In addition to constructing the website to promote the idea of eco-eating, I also analyzed visitor browsing behavior. Ultimately, this project received the highest score in the course.
I used WIX, HTML, and CSS to build the website. I conducted desktop research to understand that warm tones and a simple layout style encourage users to stay on the website. The website's name, "Eco-eating," was chosen as it combines the English words "eco" and "eating." Furthermore, I embedded Google Analytics code into my website, allowing me to analyze visitor data and make improvements.
Subsequently, I promoted the website on platforms like Dcard and Facebook groups. The data clearly showed that once I promoted the website on social platforms, the number of visitors significantly increased. In the end, the website collected data for two weeks, with approximately 2,600 visitors in total, far exceeding my expectations. This project also received recognition from the professor. It marked the beginning of my interest in web design.
